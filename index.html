DISPELLING MYTHS ABOUT

REMITTANCES

14% of the 5000 households surveyed in the World food program study gave consent about receiving remittances.

These 709 households are represented by the bubbles in the figure on the left. Each bubble represents a household.

Hover over the bubbles to discover information about the income of a household and the remittances they receive.

The most common household expenses that remittances cover include:
  • Food
  • Health
  • Education
  • Utilities
  • Clothing
  • Housing
